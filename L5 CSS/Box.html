Car

Ferrari, the iconic Italian sports car manufacturer, is synonymous with luxury, performance, and a rich racing heritage. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939, the brand has become a symbol of speed and innovation, consistently pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering. Each Ferrari model, from the classic 250 GTO to the modern-day LaFerrari, embodies a perfect blend of art and technology. Renowned for its distinctive prancing horse logo and its signature red color, Ferrari has built a legacy on the racetrack with numerous victories in Formula 1 and other motorsport events.
